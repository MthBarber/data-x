Data X Carbon Calculator

Here at Data X, we want to make a difference and that starts with us helping you. Here is a calculator to help calculate how much carbon you are creating on your journeys, use this tool to help you identify where you can make changes in reducing your carbon footprint.

Then with this knowledge, help it empower you to make better decisions for yourself, the planet and our future, so with that in mind this is a tool for everyone.

If you are are not a Data X employee, feel free to check out our carbon offsetting program, that every employee is involved with as we bid to reduce our footprint to net-zero.
